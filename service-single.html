MEP (mechanical, electrical & plumbing) Services

Construction projects involve many complex phases that need to be executed with adequate planning and precision. There shouldn’t be any compromise when it comes to quality, durability and sustainability. We at Naxos make sure all these are met with our team of experts and industry leaders.

We provide the mechanical, electrical & plumbing aspects and components required in a construction project. We consult specialised MEP engineers to choose the best design and an adept team for its installation. Under the mechanical category, we have its HVAC equipment, installation, erection & ducting along with fabrication and insulation. In the electrical category, we provide its designing, lighting & power distribution system, electrical substation, installation of switchgear, data cabling etc. Under plumbing services, we undertake installation, erection & insulation along with plumbing and drainage works and wastewater treatment. We also provide testing, commissioning and mechanical aspects of the fire fighting sector. In MEP, we go through the in-depth design and selection of these systems, which involves computer-aided design and 3D modelling to examine their structure & functionality and up-to-date documentation. We look into its technical safety and standards during installation.

Under MEP works we consider factors such as its design and functionality, prevention of location clashes, making it within the budget and performing the proper installation with proper consultation as this area is interrelated to many other construction sectors.

Mechanical

  • Designing

    • Designing plays a crucial role in the mechanical aspects of construction. It is important for planning, accurate documentation, performance and cost estimation. This sector is looked after by a team of engineers specialising in plans, prints, design specifications and experience in the MEP areas. We design parts, components, products & systems of mechanical nature. These are designed by considering its functions, safety, reliability & maintenance. The entire process is done through consultation with the electrical and plumbing service areas as these sectors are interrelated in many aspects.

  • HVAC equipment (chillers & DX)

    • Mechanical services mainly deal with HVAC in residential and commercial buildings. It includes air conditioning system, space heating and ventilation. These systems are mandatory in construction as they control the humidity and indoor temperature and supply fresh air through ventilation. Services under HVAC include furnaces, heat pumps, ductwork and other home comfort services. Its significance comes down to providing good indoor air quality. HVAC works are done by considering equipment capacity & quality, Size and compatibility, installing heat distribution systems correctly for air ducts, steam piping, refrigerant lines etc. Ensuring indoor air quality requires the removal of moisture, odour, smoke, airborne bacteria through the correct systems and apparatus. Our team of experts keep in mind the nine essential parts of an HVAC system namely air return, exhaust, filter, ducts, electrical elements, outdoor units, coils and blower. Along with HVAC installation and services, we also provide maintenance services such as clean coils and filters, emergency repairs, and check-ups to function longer and efficiently. We make sure the three essentials are met by our HVAC services. Keeping you warm during winters, cool during summers and making sure your indoor air quality is fresh to keep you healthy and safe. Our team will provide the best work and maintenance by working with modern equipment and methods under the correct guidelines so that there are no air quality issues in the future.

  • Ducting fabrication & insulation

    • We provide ductwork fabrication for the processing equipment and ducts that are an integral part of the HVAC system. Since it’s an important part of HVAC, it ensures an adequate supply of conditioned air throughout the house. There should be a proper consideration while choosing the type of ducts needed for various systems. We have fibreglass lined air ducts, fibreboard air ducts, sheet metal air ducts and flexible air ducts. Our team helps you to choose the right ductwork for your cooling system. We provide the correct size ductwork for your building needs, we make sure there is an equal supply of airflow in & out. We look into common & effective design while choosing a duct configuration along with ductwork installation and insulation. In HVAC ducting we use galvanised steel or aluminium and are commonly lined with fibreglass for insulation. It needs to have a good tolerance level to maintain system pressure which affects its efficiency and performance. We work by the saying that the support system holding the ductwork in place makes all the difference.

  • Testing and commissioning

    • Since we undertake all phases of a construction project, project commissioning and testing is an integral part of our services. Through it, we ensure all the structural components and systems of a construction project are designed, installed, tested, operated and maintained according to the requirements of the client. It is usually done after the installation phase to ensure everything is performing its function efficiently. The objective of this stage is to hand over the completed project to the client by guaranteeing its safety, performance and an orderly list of components and works done for future use. This is a crucial process and our team ensures that a construction project & its systems function as intended during the design phase and deliver the required performance and checkups are done without leaving any detail for further consideration.

  • Fire fighting

    • A project is only complete if it has the required safety apparatus installed to save the occupants in it. A project should be able to counter any safety challenges and we make sure that it is done through careful consideration by providing fire fighting systems and installation services. Under this service, we provide fire storage tanks for water storage, a specialised pumping system and a network of pipes either hydrants or sprinklers. We use galvanised steel pipes coated to prevent corrosion. We provide both the automatic wet distribution system and the automatic dry system. We also provide a manual distribution system depending on certain building codes. When it comes to complex projects and installations, our team is equipped to provide foam systems and velocity water spray systems. We take into consideration all the safety requirements of a project to ensure it’s ready for its occupants. We provide oreodo fire alarm services along with our fire fighting apparatus installation services.

Electrical Contracting

  • Designing

    • We have specialised engineers under our wing to design various electrical components and systems using various software for your construction purposes. While designing we keep in mind its functionality, the needs it has to deliver and how to optimise its overall performance. Electrical designing involves planning, creating, testing and supervising the installation and development of electrical equipment including lighting, power systems and distribution along with communications infrastructure. During the designing process, the control systems of devices such as batteries and their functionality are also considered. We undertake electrical designing projects of various capacities to go on to the next level of the project’s construction phases.

  • Lighting and power distribution system

    • Apart from electrical designing, we also provide lighting and power distribution systems integral to any construction project. We undertake radial, loop and network power distribution systems according to the client’s need and project requirement. We have a basic power distribution system for smaller buildings and provision of higher electrical load for huge buildings and projects with multiple transformers.

    When it comes to lighting we have ambient lighting for general purposes and for a uniform source of illumination. We provide task lighting where brighter light for a specific focal point is required. And accent lighting for highlighting a certain feature or to get the desired effect for a space. Under these categories, we provide the installation of chandeliers, wall-mounted fixtures, track lights, spotlight, hanging fixtures, pendant lighting, directional gimbal, tape and extrusion to name a few. In our lighting projects, we go from generic to specific lighting approaches so that the lighting need of a particular space is met. We have lighting solutions with various types to choose from to enhance the ambience of your abode.

    Power distribution depends on the building type, the loads required and the dimension and length of supply cables. We provide vertical and horizontal supply systems for rising mains and each floor level. We have various arrangements to choose from such as radial and loop system, secondary selective system, sparing transformer system, composite systems and secondary spot network. We will help you choose the best arrangement for the electrical distribution system as it has an impact on the reliability and maintenance of the systems.

    Under electrical substation, we have a subsidiary station which regulates voltage to take care of system voltage changes. We undertake the setting up of substations of varying scales such as step-up, primary, distribution, industrial, converter substations etc. we ensure the highest reliability and performance by reducing the heat, eddy currents and other transmission losses. We make it easier to distribute electricity and make the supply safe and easy.

    Installation of switchgear is an important part of controlling an electrical power system. Under switchgear devices, we have fuses, circuit breakers, isolators, relays, potential transformers, lightning arresters etc. it is directly linked to the supply system and its main function is to regulate the equipment for testing, maintenance and clearing the fault. We provide protection to the lines to prevent damage due to heavy current flow. We undertake both outdoor and indoor type switchgear.

    Transformers and generators are an integral part of any electrical power system as they generate current and convert between current and voltage. We will ensure it has good mechanical strength to counter short circuit challenges and will also make sure the heating condition of the windings satisfy standard requirements. We provide transformers with an efficient core to do the work. We have different types of transformers to choose from. In the digital age, keeping your information secure is of utmost priority. We also provide UPS security to keep your hardware, data centres and telecommunication equipment safe and secure so that your personal information is not at risk. Installing a UPS can be of benefit as they protect against power problems, security and data breach. We will help you in deciding which UPS is right for your requirement depending on the loads it needs to protect all by testing your critical power system. HV/LV is important for a large scale project or establishment as it alters the voltage according to the requirement of the equipment. It gives you an efficient energy system as it switches between high and low voltages.

    The HV/LV installation comes along with our transformers and switchgear installation if required as it is considered an electrical upgrade. If a complex electrical power system is in place then HV/LV should be considered as it will increase the performance of the system.

    Structured data cabling is a significant part of a robust telecommunications infrastructure. It should ensure efficient transmission of data from the source to the destination. When it comes to data cabling solutions we have a wide range to choose from. We also provide fibre optics, CAT specifications, voice and data projects, and switches and routers under this category. We have a team well equipped to handle complex data cabling infrastructure projects.

Plumbing

  • Designing, erection, installation and insulation

    • A building needs to have a proper waste management apparatus in place. Good plumbing services ensure convenience and comfort. It is mandatory to have safe and clean water in homes. Under plumbing works, we look after the equipment design suitable for the client’s and building’s needs. We undertake erection and installation for distribution of water and removal of wastewater and installation of a systematic sewage system. We use pieces of equipment and pipes made of non-corrosive, durable and stainless steel material to ensure the longevity of the apparatus set in place. Under plumbing and drainage works we undertake sanitation works, internal and external drainage systems, checking, testing and prevention of leakage along with proper maintenance of the system. We perform single and double stack systems. We have C.I/P.V.C/A.C. pipes for various purposes. Along with these, we also have services related to underwater and overhead tanks and plumbing system installation for fire fighting systems.

  • Chilled water piping and wastewater treatment

    • Chilled water piping plays a vital role in cooling a building’s air and equipment. This system involves HVAC equipment designed to exchange heat under specified needs and to absorb the heat from the building. We use the best stainless steel and carbon steel pipes with varying thicknesses according to the requirements. We provide chilled water system for both residential, commercial and industrial applications. This service has become mandatory for modern buildings as they act as centralised cooling systems for multiple buildings. When it comes to the insulation of chilled water pipes, we use fibreglass as it’s easy on the budget with a good degree of thermal resistance.

    Wastewater treatment is of utmost necessity in a world becoming more conscious about the planet, to have safe and healthy water and environment all around you. It's a process that removes waste and contaminants from the water and converts it into a form that is usable. This process makes the water reusable for various purposes and it is safe for the planet. We treat wastewater from various sources with strict guidelines, methods and processes by making it undergo all the three stages of wastewater treatment. We implement modern water treatment that comes along with benefits such as low maintenance, environment friendly, and less waste. Under this service we also perform sewage treatment, industrial wastewater treatment by making it undergo the required biological and chemical processes. We ensure the end result is safe and usable without harming the planet.
