Civil & structural contracting.

When it comes to our civil & structural contracting services, we implement your visions into reality with our well-equipped team and resources catering to a wide variety of needs and constructional requirements. We are equipped to handle any project from its initial stages till its completion with the utmost concern for quality, budget, employing the right methods and completing it within the timeframe.

We have a wide range of services to choose from in the area of civil and structural contracting that covers all the important phases of construction projects. We provide services related to the erection of structural steelworks, installation & assembly of a system or unit and several types of insulation methods to choose from. We also perform demolition works complying with the safety standards and painting works to match your required needs. We have specialised equipment for tasks such as concrete cutting & coring. We provide metal fabrication focusing mainly on aluminium for best results & quality. Our team also carry out interior specification works such as fit-out and furnishing along with exterior works such as landscaping & constructional cleaning so that our clients can experience a hassle-free service from the beginning till the end of a project. All the services are performed by a specialised team with an eye for detail and precision.

We understand construction projects have a complicated nature and that it needs to be executed without any compromise when it comes to meeting quality and industry standards. We have trained experts on each matter for consultation. We undertake maintenance, construction & designing of all aspects of a project to ensure the highest quality, commitment & completion within the timeframe. We are committed to making the city you live in to reflect your identity and vision of the world. We are keen on delivering services that guarantee durability, sustainability and effectiveness.

Erection, installation & insulation

    A strong foundation ensures longevity. We provide services in the initial stages of construction such as the erection of structural steelworks by ensuring that it meets safety and buildability standards. One of the key factors that provide durability is the erected frame’s positional accuracy, which we are keen on delivering with our expertise and resources. We do positioning, aligning and securing for erecting the structural frame. We execute it with proper planning & accuracy by using specialised cranes for varying heavyweight components. Carbon steel of the required quality is used in various construction & erection purposes.

    We also provide installation works related to construction such as installing machinery, plant, apparatus of a system or a unit with its necessary accessories and required assembly according to the highest standards. We undertake complex installation projects by following the positioning, supports & fixings in the installation drawings. We also do plumbing & drainage installation, fire & security apparatus installation, electrical wiring & fittings along with air conditioning & heating installation. Our team consists of licensed workers that are apt for the various types of installation works we undertake.

    When it comes to civil construction we need to make it livable by implementing the proper infrastructure and methods. Insulation is important for achieving thermal comfort for our homes. We provide insulation services such as soundproofing insulation, thermal and electrical insulation with the appropriate materials to perform the task. Thermal insulation provides energy efficiency and comfort. It also provides soundproofing to an extent. Electrical insulation plays a vital role as it provides high resistance in any electrical system. We have several types of insulation to choose from depending on where it is to be insulated such as fibreglass insulation, cellulose for environment-friendly insulation etc. we provide insulation for various types of spaces by using the required insulation methods which get the job done.

Structural steelwork

    Structural steel is the most basic & ubiquitous component in construction which has to be chosen after several quality considerations. We undertake structural steelwork that meets the industry standards regarding its size, chemical composition and mechanical properties to ensure rigidity and durability. We have different types of structural shapes to meet your construction requirements. Steel provides strength to the structure and being one of the essential components in construction, we make sure the best is chosen for your projects.

    Steel is used for its excellent strength to weight ratio. It can form desired shapes for construction. The different forms of structural steelwork we provide include standard hot rolled sections, universal beams and columns, joists, channels, hollow & flat sections etc. We use carbon steel for varying levels of quality & flexibility, medium carbon steel specifically for structural steelwork. We use galvanized steel with zinc coating to prevent corrosion and alloy steel to provide maximum rigidity & strength. Our team of experts ensure the right metal and composition is used to avoid structural problems in the future.

    As the backbone of any structure, we make sure it’s of the highest quality and its sustainability is not compromised. We have workers that have a good understanding of the composition & performance of the metals for undertaking projects in various sectors of construction. The steel frame can also be modified according to its constructional requirements and uses. And because of its eco-friendly properties, the use of steel for its strength and convenience continues to increase.

    Structural steelwork is advantageous due to its longevity, versatility and space maximisation.

Aluminium & steel fabrication

    Metal fabrication is used to create various components with varying qualities for different construction applications. The construction industry relies mostly on steel fabrication due to its favourable properties. There are different methods of fabrication that can be used depending on the requirement. We provide metal fabrication by using common and efficient methods such as cutting, forming, punching, shearing, stamping & welding for maximum durability and rigidity. We have different steel compositions such as carbon, alloy, stainless & tool steels for your required purposes.

    Aluminium fabrication has a higher strength-to-weight ratio and we do it with certain methods (such as extrusions, forming, casting, forging etc) that work better than others. Due to its high malleability, aluminium fabrication is being widely used for various component production & construction purposes. It can also be combined with other metals to create alloys for specific needs. Some of its advantages include non-toxicity, non-combustible and non-corrosive nature. We provide aluminium fabrication for various structural and architectural applications.

    When it comes to fabrication, our team works closely with steel detailers and drafters to complete the given projects with precision. We have experienced personnel that go through each stage of the process ensuring quality throughout and uses the right manufacturing techniques to deliver the best product & services that can withstand years. We have the capability to undertake projects of any scale with our expertise and resources.

Fit-out and furnishing

    We make interior spaces livable according to your aspirations. We have services that cater to the interior specifications of our clients such as fit-out and furnishing. We work along with our client or occupant to determine the kind of refurbishment that is required on a project. Our team is well versed with the latest methods and modern trends to suit your project needs. We design spaces that reflect our client’s or project’s identity.

    All work is done by consulting the client on every stage of progress. We make sure that the interior spaces we create resonate with your ideas. We transform your spaces into abodes that reflect warmth and comfort by following modern trends in the industry.

Constructional cleaning & landscaping

We do both small and large-scale post construction clean up which include removal of debris, threshold cleaning, removal of scuff marks from surfaces, power washing, etc all done by professional cleaners with the right equipment following safety measures of the occupant and area.

    We also provide landscaping services such as sod work, interlocking, installing garden retaining walls etc. we provide both softscape and hardscape landscape construction services with a team that maximises the appeal and feel of your projects. We have installation & landscaping services involving plants & other organic materials under softscaping. In hardscaping, we provide all types of stonework, garden retaining walls, firepits, patios, pool decks & fencing among many other services.
